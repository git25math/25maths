Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase a product?

Click "Buy Now" on any product page. You'll be directed to our secure checkout. After payment, you'll instantly receive download links via email.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal through our secure payment processor.

Can I print the resources?

Yes! Our PDFs are optimized for printing. Print as many copies as you need for personal/classroom use.

Do you offer refunds?

As digital products are delivered instantly, all sales are final. However, we take quality seriously — if you find any errors or issues, email us and we'll make it right. We also encourage you to try our free resources first to make sure our style suits your needs.

What if I find an error?

Email us at support@25maths.com with your order number. We'll send corrected files within 24 hours.
