# Free Kahoot Content Design (CIE 0580 + Edexcel 4MA1)

## Free unit design standard (recommended)
- Questions per free Kahoot: 12
- Difficulty split: 5 easy + 5 medium + 2 challenge
- Objective split:
  - 60% core skill fluency
  - 30% exam-style application
  - 10% common-mistake diagnosis
- Time per question:
  - Easy: 20s
  - Medium: 30s
  - Challenge: 45s
- Option style: 4 options, 1 correct + 3 misconception distractors

## CIE 0580 free items (8)
1. C1.4 Fractions, Decimals and Percentages
- Skill focus: convert and compare FDP forms
- Misconception check: place value shift, wrong percentage base

2. C1.13 Percentages
- Skill focus: percentage increase/decrease, reverse percentages
- Misconception check: additive vs multiplicative change

3. C2.5 Equations
- Skill focus: one-step/two-step linear equations
- Misconception check: sign errors and balance method mistakes

4. C3.3 Gradient of Linear Graphs
- Skill focus: gradient from points and from graph
- Misconception check: x/y difference order errors

5. C5.2 Area and Perimeter
- Skill focus: formula selection and unit consistency
- Misconception check: perimeter vs area confusion

6. C8.1 Introduction to Probability
- Skill focus: probability scale, simple event probability
- Misconception check: impossible/certain event inversion

7. E4.7 Circle Theorems I
- Skill focus: angle rules in circles
- Misconception check: wrong theorem mapping

8. E6.2 Right-Angled Triangles
- Skill focus: trigonometric ratio choice and setup
- Misconception check: opposite/adjacent/hypotenuse mismatch
