## Practice (10)
1. Simplify `6x + 2x`.
2. When `x = 5`, evaluate `6x - 2`.
3. Expand `6(x + 7)`.
4. Solve `6x + 6 = 36`.
5. Rearrange `y = 6x + 6` to make `x` the subject.
6. Solve simultaneously: `x + y = 15`, `x - y = 5`.
7. Solve `x^2 - 8x + 15 = 0`.
8. Solve the inequality `3x - 13 > 0`.
9. A recipe uses sugar and flour in ratio `3:5`. If sugar is `24 g`, find flour.
10. Given `x^2 - 7x + 12 = 0`, solve and state both roots.
