## Practice (10)
1. Find third angle in a triangle with angles `61°` and `34°`.
2. Find sum of interior angles of a `6`-sided polygon.
3. State the order of rotational symmetry of a regular hexagon.
4. Find perimeter of rectangle `8 cm` by `12 cm`.
5. Find area of circle radius `3 cm` in terms of `pi`.
6. Use Pythagoras: legs `7` and `10`.
7. In a right triangle, `sin(theta)=0.5`. Find `theta` (acute).
8. Find volume of cuboid `8 x 10 x 12`.
9. Two similar shapes have scale factor `3`. Area scale factor?
10. A cylinder has radius `3` and height `8`. Write volume in terms of `pi`.
