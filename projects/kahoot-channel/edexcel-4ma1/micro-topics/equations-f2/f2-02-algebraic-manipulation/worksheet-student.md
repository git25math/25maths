## Practice (10)
1. Simplify `4x + 1x`.
2. When `x = 9`, evaluate `3x - 2`.
3. Expand `5(x + 5)`.
4. Solve `4x + 2 = 38`.
5. Rearrange `y = 4x + 2` to make `x` the subject.
6. Solve simultaneously: `x + y = 12`, `x - y = 2`.
7. Solve `x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0`.
8. Solve the inequality `5x - 6 > 0`.
9. A recipe uses sugar and flour in ratio `3:5`. If sugar is `24 g`, find flour.
10. A rectangle has width `x+2` and length `x+5`. If area is `130`, find `x`.
