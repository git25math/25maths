3. Expand `3(x + 4)`.

5. Rearrange `y = 4x + 6` to make `x` the subject.
6. Solve simultaneously: `x + y = 14`, `x - y = 4`.

8. Solve the inequality `2x - 6 > 0`.
9. A recipe uses sugar and flour in ratio `3:5`. If sugar is `24 g`, find flour.
