3. Expand `5(x + 7)`.

5. Rearrange `y = 5x + 5` to make `x` the subject.
6. Solve simultaneously: `x + y = 15`, `x - y = 7`.
7. Solve `x^2 - 10x + 24 = 0`.
8. Solve the inequality `4x - 12 > 0`.
9. A recipe uses sugar and flour in ratio `3:5`. If sugar is `24 g`, find flour.
