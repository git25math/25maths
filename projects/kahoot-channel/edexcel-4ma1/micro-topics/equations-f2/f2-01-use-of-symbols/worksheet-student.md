3. Expand `3(x + 6)`.

5. Rearrange `y = 3x + 8` to make `x` the subject.
6. Solve simultaneously: `x + y = 16`, `x - y = 4`.
7. Solve `x^2 - 9x + 8 = 0`.
8. Solve the inequality `4x - 6 > 0`.
9. A recipe uses sugar and flour in ratio `3:5`. If sugar is `24 g`, find flour.
