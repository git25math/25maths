3. Expand `3(x + 3)`.

5. Rearrange `y = 5x + 7` to make `x` the subject.
6. Solve simultaneously: `x + y = 21`, `x - y = 3`.
7. Solve `x^2 - 11x + 30 = 0`.
8. Solve the inequality `2x - 9 > 0`.
9. A recipe uses sugar and flour in ratio `3:5`. If sugar is `24 g`, find flour.
