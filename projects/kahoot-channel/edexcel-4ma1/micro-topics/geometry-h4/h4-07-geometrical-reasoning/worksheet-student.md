## Practice (10)
1. Find third angle in a triangle with angles `70°` and `60°`.
2. Find sum of interior angles of a `5`-sided polygon.
3. State the order of rotational symmetry of a regular hexagon.
4. Find perimeter of rectangle `13 cm` by `17 cm`.
5. Find area of circle radius `6 cm` in terms of `pi`.
6. Use Pythagoras: legs `6` and `5`.
7. In a right triangle, `sin(theta)=0.5`. Find `theta` (acute).
8. Find volume of cuboid `13 x 15 x 17`.
9. Two similar shapes have scale factor `3`. Area scale factor?
10. A cone has radius `6` and height `16`. Write volume in terms of `pi`.
