## 1. What the syllabus expects
- Convert fluently between fractions, decimals, and percentages.
- Calculate percentages of amounts and percentage change.
- Solve reverse percentage questions.
- Work accurately with negative numbers.

## 2. Common exam question styles
- Direct conversion (FDP triangle).
- Multi-step percentage increase/decrease.
- Original value from final value after a percentage change.
- Mixed-format comparison and ordering.

## 3. Model example (worked)
A value is increased by 10% and becomes 132. Find the original value.
- Step 1: `132` is `110%` of original.
- Step 2: Original `= 132 / 1.10`.
- Step 3: Original `= 120`.
Final answer: `120`.
