# 25Maths Kahoot Warm Palette System (12 Topic Themes)

## Reference Notes (Research Base)
- Popular palette tooling and trend references reviewed:
- Coolors: https://coolors.co/palettes/trending
- Adobe Color: https://color.adobe.com/trends
- Color Hunt: https://colorhunt.co
- Canva color palettes: https://www.canva.com/colors/color-palettes/

Design rule used for this project:
- Warm pastel dominant, high readability, minimal decoration.
- No black frame or dark border.
- Keep text contrast with soft navy (`#4F7398` family), not pure black.

## Color Roles
- `bg`: page background
- `top_strip`: top bar
- `pill_primary`: left badge
- `pill_secondary`: board badge
- `pill_accent`: right badge (`QUIZ + WORKSHEET`)
- `panel`: main content card
- `title`: key point headline
- `subtitle`: supporting line
- `accent_line`: underline/divider
- `footer`: bottom strip
