3. State the order of rotational symmetry of a regular hexagon.
4. Find perimeter of rectangle `14 cm` by `18 cm`.
5. Find area of circle radius `7 cm` in terms of `pi`.
6. Use Pythagoras: legs `5` and `10`.
7. In a right triangle, `sin(theta)=0.5`. Find `theta` (acute).
8. Find volume of cuboid `14 x 16 x 18`.
9. Two similar shapes have scale factor `3`. Area scale factor?
