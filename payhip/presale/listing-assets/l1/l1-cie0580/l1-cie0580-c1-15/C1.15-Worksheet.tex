\begin{enumerate}[resume, label=\qnum{\arabic*}, leftmargin=3.2em, itemsep=12pt]
  \item A train leaves at 09:35 and arrives at 11:20. Find the journey time.\MarkOne \AnswerBox
  \item A lesson starts at 13:15 and ends at 14:05. How long is the lesson?\MarkOne \AnswerBox
  \item A movie starts at 6:40 pm and ends at 8:25 pm. Find the duration.\MarkOne \AnswerBox
  \item A journey takes 2 h 30 min. It starts at 07:50. What time does it finish?\MarkOne \AnswerBox
  \item A bus leaves at 16:45 and arrives at 18:05. How long is the journey?\MarkOne \AnswerBox
\end{enumerate}

  \item A shop opens at 09:00 and closes at 17:30. How long is it open?\MarkOne \AnswerBox
  \item A flight leaves at 22:40 and lands at 01:15 the next day. Find the flight time.\MarkOne \AnswerBox
  \item You need 35 minutes to get ready and 25 minutes to travel. You must arrive by 07:30. What is the latest time to start getting ready?\MarkOne \AnswerBox
