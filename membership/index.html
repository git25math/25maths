FAQ

What does rolling membership mean?

You receive all resources released while your subscription is active. New deliveries pause when the subscription is inactive.

How often are new packs released?

Weekly. We recommend publishing on a fixed day and sending one member update email after release.

I did not receive this week's email. What should I do?

Check spam/promotions first. If not found, contact support@25maths.com and include your subscription email.
