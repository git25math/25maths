Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about 25Maths

What is 25Maths?

25Maths is an online exam preparation platform specialising in IGCSE Mathematics. We currently support CIE 0580 (Cambridge International) and Edexcel 4MA1 exam boards. The platform offers interactive exercises, topical worksheets, Kahoot-style revision games, and a membership programme with weekly practice packs.

What exam boards do you cover?

We cover two major IGCSE Mathematics exam boards:

  • CIE 0580 — Cambridge International IGCSE Mathematics (Core and Extended)
  • Edexcel 4MA1 — Pearson Edexcel International GCSE Mathematics (Foundation and Higher)

Content is aligned to the latest syllabus for each board.

How does the subscription work?

The 25Maths subscription is a monthly membership at $9.99/month. As an active member, you receive:

  • Weekly topic practice packs (PDF with worked solutions)
  • Full access to all interactive exercises with progress tracking
  • Streak freeze (protect your daily practice streak)
  • Achievement system with XP and levels
  • Parent/guardian progress dashboard

You can cancel anytime. New resources released during your active period remain accessible.

Is 25Maths free to use?

Yes! A significant portion of 25Maths is free:

  • All interactive exercises are free to use
  • Kahoot-style revision quizzes are free
  • Free vocabulary card sets for both exam boards
  • Free sample worksheets
  • Blog articles with revision tips

The paid subscription adds weekly practice packs, progress tracking, streak features, and achievement gamification.

How can I track my child's progress?

We offer a dedicated Parent Dashboard where you can see:

  • Weekly summary: sessions completed, questions answered, accuracy rate
  • Topic-by-topic performance breakdown with trends
  • Weak areas that need attention
  • Achievement milestones and XP progress
  • Recommendations for next week's practice

Access the parent dashboard at /membership/parent-dashboard.html. A Chinese version is also available at /zh-cn/membership/parent-dashboard.html.

Is 25Maths suitable for self-study?

Absolutely. 25Maths is designed for independent study. Every interactive exercise provides instant feedback, and all practice packs include fully worked solutions. The streak system and achievement badges help students stay motivated without external supervision.

Many students use 25Maths as a complement to school classes or private tutoring, but it works equally well as a standalone revision tool.

Do you support bilingual English-Chinese content?

Yes. 25Maths is the only IGCSE Maths platform offering bilingual English-Chinese support. This includes:

  • Chinese parent dashboard (家长仪表盘)
  • Chinese parent landing page with platform overview
  • Bilingual blog articles covering revision strategies
  • Chinese UI labels and achievement descriptions

This is particularly useful for Chinese-speaking families studying at international schools.

What are interactive exercises?

Interactive exercises are browser-based practice questions aligned to specific subtopics in the CIE 0580 and Edexcel 4MA1 syllabuses. Each exercise:

  • Presents questions one at a time with a progress bar
  • Gives instant correct/incorrect feedback after each answer
  • Tracks your score and records your session data
  • Links to related Kahoot quizzes and worksheets

No app installation needed — they work in any modern browser on desktop, tablet, or phone.

What is the refund policy?

Payments are processed through Payhip. If you are not satisfied with the subscription, you can cancel at any time from your Payhip account. You will retain access until the end of your current billing period.

For individual product purchases (worksheets, exam prep books), Payhip offers refunds within 30 days of purchase. Contact us at support if you have any issues.

Do you offer group/school licences?

Yes. We offer B2B plans for tutoring centres and international schools. Our institutional plans include:

  • Teacher dashboard with class-level analytics
  • Assignment creation and tracking
  • Student progress reports for each class
  • Bulk pricing starting from $3/student/month

Visit our institution page or contact us for a custom quote.
