常见问题 — 关于 25Maths 平台的解答

What is 25Maths?

25Maths is an online exam preparation platform specialising in IGCSE Mathematics. We currently support the CIE 0580 (Cambridge International) exam board. The platform offers topical worksheets, Kahoot-style revision games, and a membership programme with weekly practice packs.

What exam boards do you cover?

We currently cover one major IGCSE Mathematics exam board:

  • CIE 0580 — Cambridge International IGCSE Mathematics (Core and Extended)

Content is aligned to the latest syllabus.

How does the Term Practice Pass work?

The 25Maths Term Practice Pass is a one-time purchase at $24.99. Pass holders receive:

  • 12 weekly topical practice packs (PDF with worked solutions)
  • 12 weekly topical practice packs with full worked solutions
  • Streak freeze (protect your daily practice streak)
  • Achievement system with XP and levels
  • Parent/guardian progress dashboard

One payment, no recurring charges. Your 12-week term starts from the date of purchase.

Is 25Maths free to use?

Yes! A significant portion of 25Maths is free:

  • Kahoot-style revision quizzes are free
  • Free vocabulary card sets for both exam boards
  • Free sample worksheets
  • Blog articles with revision tips

The Term Practice Pass ($24.99 one-time) adds 12 weekly practice packs, progress tracking, streak features, and achievement gamification.

How can I track my child's progress?

We offer a dedicated Parent Dashboard where you can see:

  • Weekly summary: sessions completed, questions answered, accuracy rate
  • Topic-by-topic performance breakdown with trends
  • Weak areas that need attention
  • Achievement milestones and XP progress
  • Recommendations for next week's practice

Access the parent dashboard at /membership/parent-dashboard.html. A Chinese version is also available at /zh-cn/membership/parent-dashboard.html.

Is 25Maths suitable for self-study?

Absolutely. 25Maths is designed for independent study. Free and paid practice packs include fully worked solutions, and Kahoot quizzes help students review topics quickly. The streak system and achievement badges help students stay motivated without external supervision.

Many students use 25Maths as a complement to school classes or private tutoring, but it works equally well as a standalone revision tool.

Do you support bilingual English-Chinese content?

Yes. 25Maths is the only IGCSE Maths platform offering bilingual English-Chinese support. This includes:

  • Chinese parent dashboard (家长仪表盘)
  • Chinese parent landing page with platform overview
  • Bilingual blog articles covering revision strategies
  • Chinese UI labels and achievement descriptions

This is particularly useful for Chinese-speaking families studying at international schools.

What do the free resources include?

Free resources are browser-accessible starter materials aligned to the CIE 0580 syllabus. They include:

  • CIE 0580 topic worksheet packs
  • Fully worked solutions
  • Kahoot free sample sets
  • Terminology and exam strategy articles

No app installation needed — download the worksheet resources or open Kahoot directly in any modern browser.
