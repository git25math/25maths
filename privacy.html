Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Information We Collect

  • Email address (when subscribing or purchasing)
  • Payment information (processed by Payhip; we never see full card details)
  • Usage data (pages visited, general analytics via GitHub Pages)

How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver purchased products
  • Send free resources you have requested
  • Notify you about new products (only if you have opted in)
  • Improve our website and resources

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Payhip (payment processing and product delivery). See Payhip's privacy policy.
  • Formspree (email forms). See Formspree's privacy policy.
  • GitHub Pages (website hosting). See GitHub's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts (typography). See Google's privacy policy.

Email Communications

  • We only send emails if you have explicitly subscribed
  • Every email includes an unsubscribe link
  • We never sell or share your email address with third parties
  • You can request deletion of your email at any time

Cookies

  • We do not use tracking cookies
  • Third-party services may set technical cookies
  • We do not use advertising or marketing cookies

Data Security

  • All payments are processed via Payhip's secure infrastructure
  • We do not store payment card information
  • Email addresses are stored securely

Children's Privacy

  • Our resources are designed for students aged 14+
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • Parents may contact us to request removal of any data

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time
  • Contact support@25maths.com to exercise any of these rights

Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this policy periodically
  • Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date
  • Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@25maths.com
