## 1. IXL -- School & District Pricing

**Market:** Global (strongest in US, Canada, Australia, UK)

| Tier | Price | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Classroom License (up to 25 students) | ~$369/year | Per classroom, single subject |
| Classroom License (up to 100 students) | ~$699/year | Scales with student count |
| School-wide (100-250 students) | $2,000-$3,000/year | All subjects |
| District-wide (500+ students) | ~$5-$10/student/year | Custom quote required |
| Reported actual cost | ~$4.25/student/year | Math + ELA combined (from a real district renewal document) |

**Key B2B Features:**
- Teacher Dashboard: Curriculum browser, Group Jam (real-time collaborative practice), assignment tracking
- Admin/District Dashboard: Time Spent visualization, Standards Proficiency Overview, LevelUp Diagnostic Assessments
- Reports: Per-student, per-class, per-school, and per-district analytics; state standards alignment
- Integrations: Clever, ClassLink, Google Classroom SSO

**White-labeling:** Not available.

## 3. Century Tech -- B2B Model

**Market:** UK (primary & secondary), international (Middle East, Asia, US expanding)

| Tier | Price | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Primary school (UK) | From £950/year per school | Varies by school size |
| Secondary school (UK) | From £1,350/year per school | Varies by school size |
| International / per-learner | $0.40-$2.00/learner/month | Significant reductions at scale |
| White-label (one-off fee) | Custom quote | Plus ongoing revenue-share |

**Key B2B Features:**
- AI Adaptive Learning: Diagnostics identify gaps; AI recommends personalized learning pathways
- Claims to save teachers ~6 hours/week through automated marking and planning
- Parent Dashboard: Real-time access to student progress reports
- Subjects: English, Maths, Science

**White-labeling / API:** **Yes -- Century Tech explicitly offers white-labeling and API integration.**
- Public JSON REST and GraphQL API with access to all data and AI recommendations
- One-off white-label fee + revenue-sharing agreement
- LTI integration support
- **KEY DIFFERENTIATOR for tutoring businesses wanting to embed AI-adaptive content under own brand**

## Key Takeaways for 25Maths B2B Strategy

### 1. Price Band: $3-$15/student/year
Most platforms land between $3-$15/student/year for school-wide licenses. 25Maths' planned **$3/student/month** ($36/year) is at the higher end but includes more value (IGCSE-specific + bilingual).

### 2. Century Tech is the White-Label Model
Century Tech is the **only major player offering white-labeling + API**. Their one-off fee + revenue-share model could inform 25Maths' approach to tutoring centre partnerships.

### 3. Save My Exams' B2B Gap
Despite 300,000+ educators and 2.5M students/month, Save My Exams has **no institutional licensing, no teacher dashboard, and no bulk pricing**. This is a direct opportunity for 25Maths.

### 4. Chinese Market Uses Hardware + Franchise Model
Squirrel AI (zero franchise fee, revenue from ¥200K hardware) and TAL (learning machines at ¥2,699) show China's tutoring market bundles hardware with software. 25Maths' pure-software approach is more scalable but must demonstrate comparable value.

### 5. Teacher Dashboard is Table Stakes
Every successful B2B platform (IXL, Sparx, Century, Mathspace) offers teacher dashboards with class-level analytics, homework assignment, and student progress tracking. 25Maths' institution dashboard and assignments page are aligned with this requirement.

### 6. Pricing Recommendation for 25Maths

| Tier | Suggested Price | Rationale |
|------|----------------|-----------|
| Starter (1 class, up to 30 students) | $5/student/month | Entry point for tutoring centres |
| School (up to 200 students) | $3/student/month | Competitive with Century Tech |
| Enterprise (200+ students) | $2/student/month (custom) | Volume discount for large schools |
| Free Teacher Account | $0 (1 year) | Follow Save My Exams model for adoption |
