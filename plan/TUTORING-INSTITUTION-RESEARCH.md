## 2. Features Tutoring Centers Pay For

### Teacher/Admin Dashboards

| Platform | Dashboard Capability |
|----------|---------------------|
| Sparx Maths | Homework hand-in summary, completion bars, trouble-spot identification, auto-grouped reteaching |
| IXL | Skills practiced, trouble spots, ready-made small groups, weekly question counts, skill proficiency |
| Century Tech | Real-time misconception identification, intervention recommendations; saves ~6 hours/week |

### Homework Assignment and Tracking
**Most valued feature by tutoring centers.** Must include:
- Assign specific topics/skills with deadlines
- Auto-grade with instant feedback
- Per-student completion tracking (who started, who finished, who skipped)
- Time-stamped data (did they cram or space it out?)

### Parent Communication
**Directly drives revenue retention:**
- **83% of institutions without standardized reporting have renewal rates below 40%**
- Auto-generated weekly/monthly reports (学情报告) are key driver of parental satisfaction
- In China, WeChat Mini Program delivery is the expected channel

### Analytics
**Key metrics institutions want:**
- Topic mastery rates (red/yellow/green heatmaps)
- Completion rates and time-on-task
- Improvement velocity over time
- Class-vs-class and student-vs-benchmark comparisons
- Exam readiness scoring / predicted grade

### White-Labeling
- exam-mate offers school-branded portals with logo/watermark
- Teachworks offers full color/logo/domain customization
- Chinese institutions strongly prefer their own branded experience

## 5. What Teachers Need for Daily Operations

### Must-Have Features (ranked by teacher demand)

1. **Homework review dashboard** — at-a-glance: who completed, who partially, who didn't start; which questions commonly missed; auto-group students by weakness
2. **Topic-by-topic mastery heatmap** — red/yellow/green across IGCSE syllabus per student
3. **Auto-generated parent reports** — weekly, including: topics practiced, accuracy, time spent, teacher comments
4. **Exam readiness scoring** — predicted grade per paper (CIE 0580 Paper 2 vs Paper 4), gap analysis to next grade boundary
5. **Quick homework assignment** — browse by IGCSE topic/subtopic, assign with one click, set deadline
6. **Real-time class monitoring** — live view during supervised practice (who's stuck, who's progressing)
7. **Scheme of learning auto-homework** — like Sparx: auto-set homework aligned to weekly teaching schedule
