# IGCSE Exam Preparation — Competitive Intelligence Report

> Prepared for: 25Maths (25maths.com)
> Date: 2026-02-27
> Source: Web research across 30+ sources

---

## 1. Save My Exams (savemyexams.com)

**Scale:** 2.5M students/month, ~17 employees, bootstrapped (founded 2016)

**Content Model:** Freemium — free tier gives 1 topic question and 5 revision notes/month. Premium unlocks 20,000+ revision notes, 10,000+ practice questions, model answers, past papers.

**Pricing (GBP):**
| Plan | Cost |
|------|------|
| Monthly | £12/month |
| 3-Month | £10/month (billed £30) |
| Annual | £4/month (billed £48/year) |

**Key Features:** Revision notes, topic questions with model answers, flashcards, mock exams, test builder, **SmartMark** (AI feedback on written answers)

**Revenue:** B2C subscriptions + advertising (108% daily revenue uplift from Publift ad optimization)

**Weaknesses:** Very aggressive paywall (5 notes/month is unusable), no adaptive learning, no step-by-step solutions, no bilingual support, static content, no gamification

**Chinese Support:** None

---

## 2. Physics & Maths Tutor / PMT Education (physicsandmathstutor.com)

**Scale:** One of UK's largest free revision sites. Registered non-profit since 2019.

**Content Model:** Primarily free, ad-supported. Past papers, mark schemes, model answers, video solutions, revision notes.

**Revenue:** Advertising (primary — heavy video/display ads), tutoring marketplace at pmt.education (tutors from £20/hour)

**Weaknesses:** Ad-heavy experience, no adaptive learning, no progress tracking, no interactivity (static PDFs), no personalization, no Chinese support

**Chinese Support:** None

---

## 3. Dr Frost Maths (drfrost.org)

**Scale:** Non-profit, used extensively in UK schools

**Pricing:**
| Tier | Cost |
|------|------|
| Individual Student | Free |
| Single Teacher (3 classes) | Free |
| School | £600 + VAT/year (60-day free trial) |

**Key Features:** 1,600+ random question generators (not static banks), gamification (points, trophies, mastery tracking), custom worksheet creation, auto-marking, student progress tracking

**Weaknesses:** Maths-only, UK-centric (GCSE focus), dated interface, no AI tutoring, no video content, no Chinese support

**Chinese Support:** None

---

## 4. Seneca Learning (senecalearning.com)

**Scale:** 10M+ users, 96% of UK secondary schools, 350,000+ teachers. **Acquired by GoStudent (edtech unicorn) Feb 2022.**

**Content Model:** Generous free tier — all courses free, unlimited. Premium adds AI features.

**Pricing:**
| Plan | Cost |
|------|------|
| Free | All courses + basic learning |
| Exam Boost | ~£12-17/month |
| Amelia AI Package | Higher tier (AI chatbot tutoring) |
| Grade Guarantee | +£5.99/month add-on |

**Key Features:** **Fully adaptive algorithm** (real-time difficulty adjustment), **spaced repetition** (cognitive science-based), **Amelia AI Tutor** (24/7 chatbot), AI-marked exam questions, parent portal (40,000+ parents)

**Weaknesses:** Text-based content (no video solutions), AI locked behind paywall, owned by GoStudent (direction may shift), no Chinese support

**Chinese Support:** None

---

## 5. GCSEPod (gcsepod.com)

**Scale:** Owned by Access Group. Claims students achieve one grade higher on average.

**Model:** B2B only — schools purchase licenses. No direct B2C.

**Pricing:** From £1/student/month (Years 10-11), other years free when school subscribes

**Key Features:** Short audio/video podcasts (3-5 min), MCQ quizzes, formative assessment, ready-made assignments, knowledge gap analytics

**Weaknesses:** B2B only (no individual purchase), UK GCSE focus (limited IGCSE), passive video learning, no adaptive algorithm, no Chinese support

**Chinese Support:** None

---

## 6. Mathspace (mathspace.co)

**Scale:** Australian-founded. Won BETT Asia AI Product of Year 2025. Virginia Board of Education approved.

**Pricing (School B2B):**
| Plan | Cost (USD) |
|------|-----------|
| Classes | $8/student/year |
| Schools | $15/student/year |
| Schools Plus | $23/student/year |

**Key Features:** **StepSmart Tutor** (AI grades each step of multi-step problems), feedback at every solution step, interactive textbooks, **MiloAI** (award-winning AI product), adaptive practice

**Weaknesses:** Primarily B2B, not IGCSE-aligned (general maths), no past paper integration, no Chinese support

**Chinese Support:** None

**Demand Evidence:**
- YK Pao School (Shanghai), YCIS (HK), Chinese International School — all run bilingual IGCSE programs
