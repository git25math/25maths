## Retired Product Line

The legacy website online exercise product line is retired. Do not restore its public routes, data catalog, player layout, runtime JavaScript, Functions endpoints, institution homework flow, or exercise telemetry schema.

Future practice, adaptive-question, or question-bank work should be planned as a new architecture with an ADR and a dedicated data model.
